Instructions

Welcome to Giphtionary, a pictionary like game with a twist! The goal of the game is to guess what the “Gifturist” is trying to communicate through the gifs that they send you. You win the round by guessing correctly.
Player 1: Use the search bar with terms that you would like your opponents to guess. Then click on the gifs to select them as clues, send the message by pressing enter, or click the 'Send Message' button. Game Begins!
Player 2: Take a guess by typing in the same search bar without selecting any gifs, once you got the right answer, then the roles swap!
